Privacy Policy for Kids Learning App

Last Updated: March 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Kids Learning App ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information from users of our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users.

Our app is designed to be completely safe for children and does not:

  • Collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Access device location
  • Access camera or microphone
  • Access contacts or photos
  • Contain any third-party advertising
  • Contain any in-app purchases

3. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Learning progress
  • Game scores
  • App preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed specifically for children aged 2-6 years. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable children's privacy laws. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

6. Security

Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to personal information.

7. Offline Functionality

Our app works completely offline after initial download. No internet connection is required to use any features, ensuring a safe, controlled environment for children.

8. Parental Control

Parents have complete control over the app. All learning progress is stored locally and can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@kidslearnapp.com
Website: https://kids-learn-fun-time.vercel.app

11. Consent

By using our app, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

This app is ad-free, purchase-free, and data-collection-free. We believe in providing a safe, educational environment for children without compromising their privacy.
